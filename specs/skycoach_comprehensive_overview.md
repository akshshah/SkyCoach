# SkyCoach

## Boarded a tourist. Landed a local.

*AI language tutor, built around your flight*

SkyCoach transforms dead flight time into the most focused language lesson of your trip. Powered by generative AI and Starlink connectivity, it builds a fully personalized curriculum around your destination, your trip purpose, and your planned activities—teaching you exactly the phrases you'll need before you land.

Not textbook grammar, but real conversations:

* Hotel check-in
* Restaurant orders
* Taxi directions
* Business meetings
* Cultural interactions

Only possible on the IFE. Never replicable on a phone.

# Complete User Flow

## 1. Boarding / Welcome Screen

* IFE detects destination from booking data.
* Passenger is greeted through loyalty profile.
* SkyCoach appears as a featured tile.
* Destination language is pre-selected.
* Users can switch to any language at any time.

---

## 2. Quick Onboarding (Under 60 Seconds)

Passengers answer three questions:

1. What is your current language level?
2. What is the purpose of your trip?

    * Business
    * Leisure
    * Family Visit
3. What are your top three planned activities?

    * Fine dining
    * Hiking
    * Sightseeing
    * Business meetings
    * etc.

This information drives the entire AI curriculum.

---

## 3. AI Generates a Personalized Lesson Plan

Using the Claude API, SkyCoach creates a destination-specific curriculum tailored to:

* Destination
* Trip purpose
* Planned activities
* Current language proficiency

### Examples

* A hiker traveling to Japan learns trail vocabulary.
* A business traveler flying to Paris learns boardroom French.
* A foodie visiting Rome learns restaurant-specific phrases.

---

## 4. Learn → Quiz → Confidence → Retry / Continue

The core SkyCoach learning loop:

1. Learn through flashcards
2. Take a quiz
3. Rate confidence
4. Retry or continue

Passengers always remain in control.

---

## 5. Pre-Landing Wrap-Up

Thirty minutes before landing:

* Personalized review session appears.
* Focuses on arrival-critical phrases:

    * Customs
    * Transportation
    * Hotel check-in
* AI prioritizes content based on previous quiz confidence and performance.

# Quiz System

## 1. Translation MCQ

Example:

> What does "Arigatou" mean?

Tests:

* Vocabulary retention
* Word recall

---

## 2. Context MCQ

Example:

> You're at a Tokyo restaurant and want the bill. What do you say?

Tests:

* Situational usage
* Practical communication

---

## 3. Pronunciation MCQ

Example:

> How do you pronounce "Gracias"?

Features:

* Four audio choices
* One correct answer
* No microphone required

Perfect for noisy cabin environments.

---

# Quiz Pass Rules

| Rule             | Outcome                              |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **70%+ Score**   | Unlock next level                    |
| **90%+ Score**   | Earn "Perfect Landing" badge         |
| **Failed Quiz**  | Targeted 2-minute relearning session |
| **All Attempts** | Retry and Continue remain available  |

### Important Principle

Passengers are never blocked from progressing.

# Confidence-Based Learning Loop

```text
Complete Quiz
      ↓
Rate Confidence
😅 Lucky | 🤔 Unsure | 😎 Confident
      ↓
AI Adjusts Future Lessons
      ↓
Score + Badge Displayed
      ↓
Retry OR Continue
```

## AI Response Matrix

| Score | Confidence       | AI Action                                |
| ----- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Pass  | 😎 High          | Advance and reduce repetition            |
| Pass  | 🤔 Unsure        | Reinforce words early in next level      |
| Pass  | 😅 Lucky         | Treat as soft fail and heavily reinforce |
| Fail  | 😎 Overconfident | Harder retry focused on gaps             |
| Fail  | 🤔 Expected      | Standard relearning session              |
| Fail  | 😅 Overwhelmed   | Easier retry to rebuild confidence       |
